opendungeon project-owned audio

These audio files are bundled as runtime game audio for opendungeon and should not be
replaced by generated or GM/world-specific audio without explicit ownership metadata.

Runtime files:
- assets/opendungeon-assets/runtime/audio/title-settings-loop.mp3
- assets/opendungeon-assets/runtime/audio/dungeon-loop.mp3

Runtime generated SFX:
- src/audio/sfxSynth.ts synthesizes short project-owned UI, dice, combat, item,
  gate, teleport, quest, book, and village cues at runtime.

Project usage:
- title-settings-loop.mp3 loops on title, menu, settings, tutorial, village, and other
  non-dungeon screens.
- dungeon-loop.mp3 loops only during dungeon gameplay and dungeon overlays.
- Synthesized SFX are loaded through OpenTUI loadSound() and are not sourced from
  third-party packs.
