# Village and meta-progression

The village is the place where dungeon consequences become long-term progress. It should feel like a practical hub, not a separate menu pasted onto the run.

## Village unlock

The portal room unlocks the village after enough progress. The village can hold houses, stations, farm plots, market sales, storage, cooking, and upgrade paths.

## Economy

Loot can be sold into village coins. Coins can become weapons, food, storage, repairs, or utility upgrades. The economy should stay deterministic enough to test and explain.

## Player identity

Co-op village state needs shared progress and individual identity. Players may have separate houses, farm permissions, profile names, and sync warnings.

## Meta limits

Meta-progression should help players recover and prepare without deleting dungeon risk. The run still needs danger, loss, and tactical decisions.
