# NPC types

NPCs give the dungeon memory and social texture. They should expose choices, warnings, tradeoffs, and consequences rather than only one-line flavor text.

## Cartographer Venn

Venn marks routes, hints at hidden rooms, and helps the player understand map risk. Their dialogue should branch between direction, secrets, and paid services.

## Wound Surgeon Iri

Iri handles healing, injury advice, and defensive rhythm. Their choices can ask whether the player wants quick healing, long-term protection, or information about enemy damage types.

## Shrine Keeper Sol

Sol trades blessings and relic lore for focus decisions. Their role is to make spiritual choices feel mechanical: spend focus now, gain a blessing, or keep resources for combat.

## Jailer Maro

Maro gives mimic warnings, lockpick branches, and trap advice. They should be useful before the player opens a suspicious chest or door.

## Ash Merchant Pell

Pell sells deterministic trade items when the run has enough gold. Merchant inventory should be tied to seed and progression so it stays testable.
